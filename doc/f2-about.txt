





                F2 - ERPXE About
                ================

                ERPXE project simplifies the process of
                installing and customizing a Multi-Boot
                PXE server with Over 100 different plugins
                available for download including:

                Windows PE , Hirens Boot CD, Fedora,
                Symantec Ghost, FOG, Ubuntu, Debian,
                CentOS, openSUSE, Gentoo, RIP Linux,
                Slackware, Backtrack, PartedMagic
                and the list just goes on...
